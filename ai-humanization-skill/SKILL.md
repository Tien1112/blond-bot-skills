AI Humanization S# AI Humanization Prompts
\## Professional Rewrite Templates for Natural Content

This file contains tested prompt templates for transforming AI-generated content into natural, human-sounding text while maintaining message integrity.

\---

\## Usage Guidelines

\*\*When to use these prompts:\*\*
\- After generating initial content with AI
\- When content feels robotic or stiff
\- Before publishing any AI-assisted content
\- To pass AI detection tools

\*\*How to use:\*\*
1\. Select the prompt that matches your need
2\. Replace \`\[paste text]\` with your content
3\. Run through Claude or your preferred AI tool
4\. Review and adjust the output
